Malpe Beach, Udupi

by Manisha R

Malpe is a natural port in the Udupi District in Karnataka, India located at the mouth of the Malpe River about six kilometers to the west of Udupi, it is an important port and a major fishing harbour on the Karnataka Coast. The town of Malpe is associated largely with settlements of the Mogaveera fisherman community.
